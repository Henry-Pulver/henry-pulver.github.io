Our Aim

To unleash the world's untapped potential by opening up the realm of higher education to anyone seeking to learn.

How?

Online learning at present fails to give meaningful feedback and reputed accreditation which make university education so valuable. We aim to provide reliable assessment and feedback in online education at scale.

Network of assessors

With the help of a network of distributed freelance assessors on our platform, university-standard assessed courses can be offered more conveniently at a fraction of the cost.

Trust

A robust machine learning model coupled with human moderation ensures our assessors’ marks and feedback can be trusted.

Interested in being an assessor?

We believe our technology will spark a revolution in education, and we want you to be at the heart of that!

Earn while you learn

Reviewing and giving feedback on others’ work is an effective way of consolidating learning [1]. Our assessors do so flexibly and can earn up to £40 per hour.
